What Guild is

A working community, not a marketplace.

Guild exists for guides — the people doing the actual work. No commissions, no skimming, no race to the bottom. Built around three things.

01

Find and share work

Post jobs when you can't cover them. Pick up jobs from guides you trust. See who's posting and who's responding — no anonymous platform middle.

02

Stay in the loop

Threads for the questions that keep coming up — routes, permits, neighbourhoods, difficult groups. People answer because they're in the room.

03

Vetted, city by city

Every member is reviewed by a city admin before joining. That's slow on purpose — it's what keeps the signal high and the conversation worth showing up for.

Two ways in

Guide or Operator.

Most members are working guides — the Guide tier is free. Tour operators and agencies who post regularly upgrade to Operator for unlimited posting, priority placement, and the full guide directory.

Guide

Free

  • Read every job post in your city
  • Post 1 job per week (Mon 00:00 local reset)
  • €2 to bump a post into the Priority lane for 24h
  • €2 to post an extra job beyond the weekly cap
  • See other guides' name, languages, city, photo
  • Forum, direct messages, in-app notifications

Operator

€10 / week

  • Unlimited job posts
  • Every Operator post is priority — pushes to every member's phone
  • Full guide profiles — response time, companies, bio, tours
  • Operator badge visible to every member
  • Cancel any time from the Stripe billing portal
