First time signing in

  1. Open the Guild app on your phone.
  2. Sign in with the email you applied with, and the temporary password from your approval email.
  3. You'll be asked to set a new password and complete a short profile — tours, languages, status. That's the on-ramp; takes a couple of minutes.
  4. Once your profile's complete, the full app unlocks.

Lost the temporary password? Use Forgot password on the sign-in screen — it'll email you a reset link.
